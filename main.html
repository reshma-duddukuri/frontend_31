About

Amity University is a prominent in India, with its main campus in Banglore and offering over 100 UG, PG, doctoral, and post-doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Sciences, and Humanities, known for strong placements, modern infrastructure with labs and research centers, and a focus on practical, research-based learning.
